### Abstract
Venture to the cursed Bloodstone Oasis, where traitors have risen as undead. Build a stronghold, gather resources, and fight back with the help of local paladins. Your goal is simple: slay as many undead as you can while surviving the relentless onslaught and cleansing the land of its curse.
